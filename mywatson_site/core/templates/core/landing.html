Keeping it simple.

Anytime, anywhere.

A simple and intuitive user interface on the web ensures that you have the best experience possible when using MyWatson by alowing you to do the things you want, quick and easy, as well as guaranteeing that you can access it whenever and wherever you want.

Upload once, forget manual labeling

Automatic Labeling powered by Google Cloud Vision™

The Google Cloud Vision™ API provides a powerful way to analize and understand the image through the power of Machine Learning, and easily classifies an image according to its contents, outputing labels.

Besides objects, it can also identify faces, text, logos, colors and landmarks, as well as searching for related concepts in the web.

Easily find who matters

Power your gallery by identifying and grouping people

By detecting people using the power of Face Detection by Google Cloud Vision™, their facial features can then be extracted by a trained Convolutional Neural Network. People are then clustered together using a Clustering algorithm, which groups sets of features by similarity.

You can then give names to a group of faces and the photos where thoses faces appear will be tagged accordingly!

About

MyWatson

MyWatson is my Master's Thesis. It is a personal web assistant focused on photos, but I think an even more general assistant has potential to be used by a lot of people as a way to ease the burden on our daily lives.

Inspired by IBM's Watson, the idea was that everyone could have their own personal Watson.
